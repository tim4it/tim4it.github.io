Tic Tac Sum Game Rules

Game HIGH SCORE with SUM:

Drag and drop provided number below board to one of the 3Ã—3 blue fields. When number is dropped into one of the filed, calculations are made - horizontal, vertical and diagonal. Sum of all calculation must be close or equal to game selected SUM number. The highest score is when horizontal, vertical and diagonal sum is equal to game SUM number.


Game LOW SCORE with SUM:

All horizontal, vertical and diagonal sum must be far (-+) of game selected SUM number for lowest score.Game ended when all fields are filled with numbers and calculations are made on the side animated fields.


In game timer:

There is also time component. More points are available for fast drag/drop play. All combination of scores are available in score board.
